Metal fabrication companies face unique challenges through varying preferences in the way components are delivered. For example, a custom manufacturer may need a metal fabricator to take on a design-on-demand project, often in the middle of production. These types of design changes occur frequently, creating the demand for a flexible cost estimate.

Companies in the ERP metal fabrication industry are implementing cost reduction strategies, which include plant closures, inventory reductions and efficiency improvements. One source of efficiency improvement is modern technology.

Recent advances in software technology and computational power have made it possible for manufacturers to execute extremely complex modeling simulations in a reasonable amount of time.
